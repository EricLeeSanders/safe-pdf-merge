Privacy Policy

Uploaded files:

Files that are uploaded to our server are removed immediately after merging. After the merged file is downloaded, the merged file and all uploaded files are deleted. Files older than 1 hour are deleted automatically every hour. No files are ever saved. Your files are private. They are never shared and never made available to anyone else.

Data we collect:

We collect basic information from you for internal purposes to know how you are using the site. We collect your IP address as well as the file size and number of pages being merged. Contents of the uploaded and merged files are never collected.
